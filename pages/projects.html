Final Group Project

through WOZ-U


During my education through WOZ-U, 4 students and myself were placed together to form a team. We had 6-weeks to complete our final project and happened to finish a week early. We were to build a full C.R.U.D operational application. We had three ideas in mind to complete this task. The first one was a COVID-19 status update, the other was a family tree, or a budget app. We ultimately decided on the budget app. After we decided on app we had to think about the technology that we would use to create the application. We decided on React, Express, and MySQL. We wanted to be able to deploy the application however we were not able to deploy it at that time.
